About FrameLink

Building and maintaining a business solution

Today, most companies need software to track and develop their businesses, but only a few can afford the resources for a consulting service to build and maintain the software they need. The reason why this is so expensive is due to the big divide between technical knowledge and business knowledge about what is needed. People with technical background take a long time to undestand customer needs while business people take a long time to understand what can be done with the current technology. Because technology innovations opens new ways to do business, creative entrepreneurs have a lot of interest in technology.

Small and micro Companies solve this problem using the most flexible tool they know - the spreadsheet. With a spreadsheet we do a model for our business and we can maintan that model as the business evolves.

But spreadsheets have great limitations. You cannot create flexible data structures, you cannot launch services from without complementary tools and you end up being the only team member able to maintain a complex set of linked spreadsheets. With complexity, spreadsheets become a huge time consumer that also limits your creativity - "if all you have is a hammer, everything looks like a nail".

We built Framelink to solve this problem. In Framelink we believe that in a near future most customers will solve their software needs by themselves. Doctors, teachers, farmers and executives know their challenges better than anyone else and they will move from problem to solution without consulting support.

As an entrepreneur you should be free to focus on your business while good software will adapt itself to your needs helping your business creativity.

Data

The information we securely share among team members to operate and develop the business. Team members have access permissions adapted to their company role.

Services

Actions we perform with company data, such as sending Emails, sending SMSs, asking for payments, check for receivables etc. Team members can execute those actions based on the permissions they have.

Collaboration

The team can have face to face meetings at any time according to each member availability.
