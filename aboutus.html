About FrameLink

Building and maintaining a business solution

Most companies need software to track and develop their businesses, but only a few can afford the time and money to build and maintain the software they need. This is so expensive because of the big divide between technical knowledge and business knowledge about what is needed. People with technical background take time to undestand customer needs while business people take time to understand technology. Technological innovations open up new business opportunities and this attracts creative entrepreneurs.

Small and Micro Companies solve this problem using the most flexible tool they know - the spreadsheet. With a spreadsheet we do a model for our business and we can maintan that model as the business evolve.

When the number of spreadsheets grow, the system becomes increasingly difficult to use and maintain. Any task execution requires complementary tools. Very often you end up being the only one able to maintain a complex set of linked spreadsheets and addons.

Framelink was build to solve this problem. We believe that in a near future most customers will solve their software needs by themselves. Doctors, teachers, farmers and executives know their challenges better than anyone else and they will move from problem to solution without consulting support. As an entrepreneur you should be free to focus on your business while good software will adapt itself to your needs helping your business creativity.

Data

The information we securely share among team members to operate and develop the business. Team members have access permissions adapted to their company role.

Services

Actions we perform with company data, such as sending Emails, sending SMSs, asking for payments, check for receivables etc. Team members can execute those actions based on the permissions they have.

Collaboration

The team can have face to face meetings at any time according to each member availability.
