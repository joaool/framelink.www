Share and Collect data with your customers and partners.


Like any big company, small companies also need to share live data with their Customers and Partners. Under your full control Customers and Partners can directly update data relevant to your business. No more manual updates via Emails.

A few examples:

  • Share invoice status with your customers.
  • Ask customers to correct their own data in your files.
  • Ask freelancers working for you for their available time slots.
  • Let customers enroll their employees in a training session you are promoting.
  • ...

This will strengthen your relationship with customers and partners. All collected data will land on the right places in FrameLink, ready for future actions.

 
Give a better service to your customers while saving your time.
